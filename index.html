How I Work

My Creative Process

01

Research & Reference

I study the game's tone, genre, and target audience before touching any tools. Mood boards, reference sheets, and competitor analysis come first.

02

Sketch & Blockout

Quick paper sketches followed by grey-box blockouts in Unreal. I test flow, sight lines, and pacing before committing to any final assets.

03

Build & Iterate

Assets built in Maya, brought into Unreal. I iterate based on playtesting feedback — level design is never done on the first pass.

04

Polish & Document

Final lighting, VFX polish, and thorough documentation of decisions made. I believe a project isn't finished until it's explained well.
